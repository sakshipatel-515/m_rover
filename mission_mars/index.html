PATHFINDING VISUALIZER

Pathfinding Visualizer allows the Rover to find and visualize the Shortest Path among all the paths between Source node and Destination node in the presence of various Obstacles after the Rover has reached Mars. The visualizer also enables us to add Crater node, Additional Station Node, a Maze or a Pattern in the grid from a variety of Mazes and Patterns and also allows us to choose the speed of the Rover. Pathfinding Visualizer also has the feature of Graphical Visualization which has been explained in the next section.

GRAPHICAL VISUALIZER

Graphical Visualizer allows your Rover to choose a Source and Destination node in the selected Graph and the Shortest path between the two is depicted through the Edges, also showing visited and unvisited nodes. You can Reset the Graph by clicking on Reset button and again choose Source and Destination Node on the same Graph and clicking on Begin button will find the Shortest Path. New Graphs can be obtained by clicking on New Graph button.
